PLACES MUST VISIT

OH MY DOG CAFE / CAFE PEFE

I had visited 20/12/2020

An absolute must visit place for pet lovers! So many adorable in-house lovelies - make sure you buy some treat for them, they're used to being pampered! Also a great place to take your pet for some spa time. And if you don't have a pet and willing to get one, they've got a great adoption service going on as well.



PUPPY CUDDLES

I wish to visit it

It is a very good place for dog lovers. They have different breeds of dogs to play with. The staff is really friendly and very good in communication. The beverages they provide are very nice in taste. But a suggestion to the cafe people...plz keep a roller to remove dog hairs from the clothes once the play session gets over. And plz provide clean washroom for use...So far the experience was really good. My daughter n her friend enjoyed a lot.
